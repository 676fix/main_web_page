Shop Stewards

A Shop Steward is an employee of a company who represents and defends the interests of her/his fellow employees as a labor union member and official.

They’re the eyes and ears of your Teamster Business Agent and any request for Union information, grievances, or copies of your contract should be addressed to them so they can get you the tools you need to succeed as an informed and active Member.
